About Me

I'm a Research Librarian for Science & Engineering at the University of Virginia in Charlottesville, VA. In my career I am looking to leverage my programming and Geographic Information System (GIS) skills

In my free time I love to be outside, especially rock climbing or skiing. I am a cat person but I like dogs too. I'm also interested in learning foreign languages, personal finance, and spending time with my girlfriend.
